Quiz will proceed in three rounds 'PRELIMS', 'IDENTIFY THE BRAND' and 'FINAL'.
ROUND-1: PRELIMS
A question paper consisting of 15 questions will be catered to all the contestants. This is a written elimination round.
ROUND-2: IDENTIFY THE BRAND
The top -12 teams from the written round will be playing 'ROUND-2'.Here a set of 10 questions based specifically on the famous brands will be presented.
ROUND-3: FINALS
Top 4 out of the 12 teams will proceed to the final round the rules of which will be explained by the quiz master at the venue itself.
