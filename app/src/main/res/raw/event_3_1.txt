Talent works, genius creates...

Objective is to design a given 2D plan of a 2BHK residential building by using Auto-CAD software only (Systems will be provided by organizing team).

The team will have to make a plan on Auto-CAD according to the plan provided at the event.

The plan may consist of top view, elevation and cross sections.
The plan will have to be completed at a specified time.
Decisions will be made by the judges on who gets to go to the final round. Judge's decision will be final.
