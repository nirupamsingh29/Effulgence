This event consists of three rounds : In the first, the qualifying round, a collection of different electronic components will be provided out of which minimum three components are to be used by the 
teams to make a working circuit. All the qualifying teams will be provided with the same task in next round. The whole task will be of 35 minutes in which starting 
15 minutes will be for thinking about completion of the task and the remaining time will be for the implementation of the circuit. In final round the qualifying teams
will be shown a video/image in which a man is trying to do something you have to figure out what exactly he wants to do.
